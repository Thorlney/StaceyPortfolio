Featured Project

SolarSense AI

AI-Powered Solar Viability Assessment for African SMEs

Platform Web App (Mobile-First)
Timeline 19 Days
Team Size 19 Members
My Role Lead Product Manager
Status Demo-Ready MVP

The Problem

600 million Africans lack reliable electricity. Solar could save businesses 30–60% on energy, but professional assessments cost $500–2,000, take 4–12 weeks, and aren't accessible to small businesses. Result: <2% solar adoption despite optimal conditions.

The Solution

SolarSense AI generates professional-grade solar recommendations in 3 minutes using 4 AI models:

  • Energy consumption estimation (±20% accuracy)
  • Solar generation prediction (±15% accuracy)
  • Financial ROI calculation (formula-based)
  • AI recommendation engine

My Role & Impact

  • Led 19-person team across 7 specialized tracks (DS, Backend, Frontend, Design, DevOps, Cyber, PM)
  • Defined MVP scope: 26 must-have features using MoSCoW framework
  • Conducted user research survey (30–50 SME owners, 3 countries)
  • Managed critical dependencies with 2-day integration buffers
  • Resolved blockers in <4 hours average (target: <24 hours)
  • Handled high-risk scenario with contingency plan activation
  • Delivered comprehensive documentation (Technical + AI Integration)

STRIDE

On-Demand Proxy Driver Service for Lagos

Platform Mobile App (iOS & Android)
Timeline Mini Capstone
My Role Product Manager & Developer
Status Concept + Working MVP

The Problem

Lagos nightlife + high car ownership = drunk driving crisis. Uber solves "how do I get home?" but not "what about my car?" People face a tough choice: drive drunk, leave car behind, or rely on unreliable friends.

The Solution

Value Proposition: "Like ride-hailing, but distinct. We don't just move you; we move you and your car home safely."

  • On-demand proxy drivers via mobile app
  • Driver drives YOUR car home (with you in it)
  • 24/7 availability for nightlife, events, family needs
  • Verified drivers with insurance partnerships
  • Distance/time-based pricing

My Contributions

  • Conducted market research: Lagos nightlife, road safety, competitors
  • Created Value Proposition Canvas mapping customer pains/gains
  • Designed user flows: booking, tracking, payment
  • Vibecoded working MVP prototype
  • Defined AI applications: driver verification, smart matching, demand prediction
  • Developed go-to-market strategy and team structure

Target Market

Lagos middle-upper class partygoers (25–45 years), car owners attending nightlife events, safety-conscious individuals who won't drink and drive.
